Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Mobile crane rent kitna hai Muscat mein? (How much is the rent for a mobile crane in Muscat?)

The rental price depends on the capacity (e.g., 25T vs. 100T) and the specific type (Truck Mounted vs. Rough Terrain). We offer flexible, highly competitive daily and weekly rates. For an exact quote, please WhatsApp our support center (+968 9192 9375).

2. What is the difference between a Truck Crane and a Rough Terrain Crane?

A truck mounted crane is designed for high-speed highway travel and quick setup on paved surfaces. A rough terrain crane is engineered with 4x4 steering and oversized tires to navigate mud, sand, and unpaved off-road sites.

3. Are your mobile cranes approved for PDO sites?

Yes, all SK Transports mobile cranes are fully certified by Petroleum Development Oman (PDO), ensuring access to high-security energy zones.

4. Do you provide certified operators with the rental?

Absolutely. We supply highly trained, internationally certified riggers and operators with our mobile cranes to maximize safety.

5. How fast can you deliver a mobile crane?

Due to our fleet's mobility and our 18+ branches, we can dispatch mobile cranes to local sites almost immediately upon contract completion.
